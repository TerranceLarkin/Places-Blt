The best BLT ever

This is my favorite BLT reciepe

You will enjoy this sandwich for years to come

Ingredients

  • bread
  • mayo
  • bacon
  • lettuce
  • tomato

Cooking Steps

  1. cook bacon
  2. chop lettuce and tomatoes
  3. spread mayo on both peices of bread
  4. stack bacon, lettuce, and tomato on one slice of bread
  5. Place other slice on top of tomato
